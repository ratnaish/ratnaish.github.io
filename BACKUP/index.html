Frequently Asked Questions

Below are various questions frequently asked by Zinny Rental Services's customers.

How many portable toilets will I need for my event?

As a rough guide, you should rent a minimum of one portable restroom for every 50 guests. Add twenty percent more units when alcohol is to be served.

What happens to the waste material from the portable chemical toilets?

The discharged waste from the porta potties is pumped from the units and transferred to a treatment facility. This waste disposal process is carried out in an environmentally friendly and sustainable manner.

Can you put toilet paper in a portable toilet?

Yes. However, only use the toilet paper we provide you.

The toilet paper we give you is specifically made to be used in portable chemical toilets. It’s more environmentally friendly and doesn't damage the internals of the toilet unit. Regular toilet paper can damage the units and pumping system.

Are portable toilets and porta potties the same?

Yes. Portable toilets are known by several names in FL, including:

Mobile restroom
